Welcome to the Minecraft Item Identifier!

This computer vision AI program can look at screenshots from Minecraft and identify certain objects inside chests, inventories, or when thrown on the ground.


Our Purpose

The Minecraft Item Identifier is useful both in-game and with screenshots. It's helpful for new players who may not easily recognize the plethora of in-game items and blocks, and for veteran players trying to navigate unorganized inventories. It's also useful in identification of far-away or hard-to-reach items.
